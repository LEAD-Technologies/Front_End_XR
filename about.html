INTRODUCING LEADXR

We facilitate the Adoption of XR Tech across Africa.

The adoption of XR in Africa is currently slow-paced, and we at LeadXR want to help speed things up. The need for increased awareness, building a community of XR enthusiasts and creators, and the facilitation of hardware purchases is critical, and this is what LeadXR is all about. We want to deliver XR experience and hardware to as many Africans as possible.

What we do and how we do it.

To achieve our mission of facilitating the adoption of XR technology across Africa, for social, economic and technological impact, we educate the masses about XR, supply affordable and sustainable XR gadgets to ensure XR is accessible, safe and inclusive for the African community and businesses. We also provide merchandise, solutions and facilitate and partner in events that promote XR in the African ecosystem.
